Escape Room Horror Game

Inspiration

I really like to play escape rooms and horror games, and solving puzzles in the escape room is a very fulfilling thing for me. I hope to bring that sense of achievement to others. Adding scary elements makes it more immersive. Sometimes people don't have the opportunity to play Escape Room offline, so I created this online horror type escape room.

Project Overview

This project is an interactive, web-based escape room game built using p5.js. Players navigate through various challenges and puzzles by interacting with on-screen elements. The game leverages unique graphics, sound effects, and logical puzzles to create an immersive and suspenseful experience. By blending horror elements with engaging gameplay mechanics, it aims to simulate the thrill of a real-life escape room in a virtual environment.

Objective: To provide players with a fulfilling and accessible escape room experience that combines puzzle-solving with the excitement of a horror atmosphere.

Features and Gameplay

  • Interactive puzzles that guide players step by step to ensure accessibility for all age groups.
  • Horror atmosphere enhanced by suspenseful music and eerie visuals to immerse players.
  • Layered gameplay with contrasting scenes that shift from warm to intense, creating a dynamic experience.

Tools and Methodology

  • Tools Used: p5.js for motion recognition, interactive visuals, and game logic; sound design tools for suspenseful music and sound effects.
  • Development Process: Designed multiple scenes with distinct visual and auditory elements to evoke different emotions. Created puzzles with intuitive progression to ensure accessibility and enjoyment for a wide audience.

Outcomes and Achievements

  • Immersive Atmosphere: Successfully created a suspenseful environment through the use of sound design, visual transitions, and interactive gameplay.
  • Accessible Gameplay: The puzzles are easy to follow and suitable for players of all ages, offering an enjoyable experience for both casual and dedicated players.
  • Creative Achievement: Demonstrated the ability to merge technical and creative elements, producing a cohesive and engaging escape room experience.

Future Directions

  • Expand the game with multiple levels offering increasing difficulty and complexity.
  • Introduce a multiplayer mode for collaborative puzzle-solving experiences.
  • Optimize for mobile platforms to allow players to enjoy the game anywhere.
  • Add more dynamic horror elements, such as jump scares and hidden clues, for extra thrill.
