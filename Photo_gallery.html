Inspiration

This photo gallery project draws inspiration from three distinct themes rooted in Chinese mythology, folklore, and culture:

  • The Green Snake: Inspired by one of the Four Great Chinese Love Legends, The Legend of the White Snake. This series focuses on the sister of the White Snake, the Green Snake, highlighting her amphibious nature and allure.
  • The Ethnic Korean Queen: Blending elements of Chinese Ethnic Korean culture with a fantastical royal narrative. The incorporation of fox symbols ties the story to the common representation of magic in Chinese mythology.
  • Peach Blossom Mythology: Based on Chinese myths involving virtual characters and set under a mystical peach tree, a symbol of romance and immortality.
These themes aim to showcase the depth of Chinese mythology while merging cultural storytelling with modern visual representation.

Story Elements

This time, I hired professional photographers and makeup artists to shoot in the photography base in Chengdu, China. Different people and stories required different scenes:

  • The Green Snake: Shot near and in water to reflect the amphibious nature of the character. Used turnip-green waisted Hanfu with a modern twist to add an alluring yet traditional vibe.
  • The Ethnic Korean Queen: The scene features a mystical queen with magical powers, highlighted by the inclusion of fox motifs, a symbol of magic in Chinese mythology. Styling involved elaborate royal attire with intricate details.
  • Peach Blossom Mythology: Set under a peach tree with blowers and petals to enhance the ethereal and mysterious ambiance.
Lighting: Each theme used unique lighting to complement the narrative: cool lighting for the Green Snake, purple lighting for the Queen, and warm lighting for the Peach Blossom scene.

Key Takeaways

This project deepened my understanding of how visual storytelling can bridge cultural heritage and modern aesthetics. It demonstrated the importance of collaboration, from stylists to photographers, to achieve a cohesive and impactful gallery. Each theme provided a unique opportunity to experiment with lighting, costumes, and set design, enhancing my creative versatility.

Challenges and Learnings

  • Challenges Faced: Coordinating multiple team members, including photographers and makeup artists, to ensure the shoots stayed on schedule. Balancing traditional and modern aesthetics to maintain authenticity while appealing to contemporary audiences. Achieving the desired atmosphere using different lighting setups for each scene.
  • Lessons Learned: Gained experience in managing multi-location shoots with varied setups and themes. Improved skills in integrating symbolism and visual effects to enhance storytelling. Developed a deeper appreciation for the technical aspects of lighting and set design in photography.

Future Directions

  • Expand the gallery to include more themes inspired by lesser-known Chinese legends and characters.
  • Explore the use of digital effects to create hybrid visual narratives that merge mythology with futuristic aesthetics.
  • Collaborate with international artists to blend Chinese mythology with global cultural influences, creating cross-cultural storytelling projects.
